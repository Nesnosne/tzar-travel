Hello! I am Natalya German-Tsarkova, an experienced tour guide and art historian. My personally vetted and experienced guides will lead you through the vast city and beyond… all in a private tour vehicle.

Ethos of Tzar Travel

I believe in providing high quality personalized service to my clients. Besides impeccable knowledge of St. Petersburg’s civic and art history, I believe that my clients should be entertained during their tours.

In 2004 I graduated from St. Petersburg Academy of Fine Arts, Dept. of Art History. I started guiding in 2005. Being an educated art historian, I never envisioned becoming a tour guide, but like many milestones in life, it happened by chance. A friend of mine asked me to show his relatives around St. Petersburg. I gave it a try and realized a wonderful sense of personal fulfillment. Sharing my knowledge and passion for this city with my clients was a real pleasure for me. Eventually, my experiences grew into «Original St. Petersburg Tours», now "Tzar Travel".

Today, my staff of ten, highly qualified, handpicked guides eagerly await our opportunity to share my vision of what a tour should be. My personal assistant Anastasia is also available to assist a smooth running program.

Note: Professional experienced drivers and high quality, comfortable vehicles is something we are very serious about.
