Day 1 City tour and Yusupov palace 9:00am-6:00pm

City tour

City tour covers all the main highlights of St.Petersburg and includes two inside visits: Peter and Paul Fortress and Peter and Paul Cathedral (burial vault of the Romanovs) and Church on Spilled Blood

Yusupov Palace

Yusupov palace belonged to one of the wealthiest families in Russia. This spectacular palace is famous not only for its interiors but also for the murder of Grigory Rasputin, which happened there in 1916.
